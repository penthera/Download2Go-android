IVideoAdPackage

interface IVideoAdPackage

The VideoAdPackage represents the full sequence of ads assciated with an asset. These may have come from a VAST definition and simply be ordered by sequence, or they may be from VMAP and contain full timing details for when each ad should be played. All of the details for playback are stored within the advert definitions themselves.
